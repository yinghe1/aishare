Agent Harness: Three Pillars

Tell it what to do. Limit what it can do. Check what it did.

1

Context Engineering

  • Feed the right information at the right time
  • Not everything — curated + dynamic context
2

Architectural Constraints

  • Rules, tests, linters, permissions
  • Prevent invalid or unsafe actions
3

Continuous Validation

  • Test outputs
  • Catch errors
  • Enforce correctness

Sample: oh-my-openagent  |  Course: oh-my-opencode-course
